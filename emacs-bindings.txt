* Possibly available bindings for mintty

C-o: open-line
C-n: next-line
C-p: previous-line
C-t: transpose-chars
C-l: recenter-top-bottom
C-\: toggle input method
C-]: abort-recur

M-a: backward sentence
M-c: capitalize word
M-e: forward sentence
M-h: select paragraph -> rebound to expand region, M-H contract region
M-i: tab to tab stop
M-j: indent new comment line
M-k: kill sentence
M-l: downcase word (to end of word)
M-m: back to indentation (move to first non-white)
M-o: face menu prefix
M-q: fill paragraph (reformat, rewrap)
M-r: move cursor to window center / top / bottom
M-t: transpose words
M-u: upcase word
M-y: yank pop (M-v dup)

M-S-[a-z] is entirely unused
M-[0-9] is numeric arguments, duplicated by C-u <numbers>



Mintty undefined:

C-%
C-!
C-#
C-$
C-%
C-&
C-*
And more probably
